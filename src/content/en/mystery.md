# The Mystery Entertainment

Stripping away the 'okazari' to observe the blueprint of truth.  
\*Seina’s life-staking play\*

[-Vegapedia-](https://en.japoneson.com/dictionary#mystery)

**March 2022.**

I moved to Shimonoseki, Yamaguchi Prefecture.

In November 2021, I pre-registered for my ashes to be scattered at Mekari Shrine, overlooking the Kanmon Straits. A few months later, sensing a global shift in food security, I decided to seek a base for self-sufficiency.

In Japan, there is a growing "Akiya" (empty house) problem. Due to a declining and aging population, many abandoned homes are sold or rented at very low prices. While searching across various prefectures, I found a very old house with a garden in Shimonoseki for only 32,000 yen (about $200) a month. In this city of steep hills, many areas are inaccessible by car, causing properties to lose their market value.

This house is a 20-minute walk from Akama Shrine. For me, having long wanted to delve into *The Tale of the Heike* before the end of my life, it was the perfect place. It was also a "DIY-OK" property—meaning I had to rebuild it myself just to make it livable.

**This is where the story, the "Mystery Entertainment," began.**

In Shimonoseki, I began to stumble upon fragments of hidden history—pieces of a puzzle that form the foundation of modern Japan, yet are difficult to speak of officially.
